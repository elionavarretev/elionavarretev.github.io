Elio Navarrete

About

QA Lead at Open Loop with over 10 years of experience in quality engineering across e-commerce, digital platforms and enterprise products. Specialist in test automation architecture using Playwright, Cypress and Serenity BDD, with a strong foundation in ISTQB methodologies.

Professor at Universidad Peruana de Ciencias Aplicadas (UPC) teaching Software Quality Engineering. Published author of a comprehensive QA book on Amazon and researcher with 4 IEEE conference papers on applied AI in education and health.

Passionate about American billiards — 8-ball, 9-ball and 10-ball.
